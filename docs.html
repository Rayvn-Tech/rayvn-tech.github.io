Overview

Rayvn is an IoT-driven autonomous rescue robotics system combining aerial drones and rugged legged ground crawlers connected via a LoRa-based mesh network. It enables offline, real-time victim and animal detection, mapping, and 2-way communication in GPS-denied environments.

“Eyes in the sky, hope on the ground.”

Mission

Rayvn bridges visibility and communication gaps during disasters using computer vision, thermal sensing, gas sensing, swarm AI, and autonomous robotics — improving situational awareness and establishing a critical voice link with survivors when every second matters.

Problem Statement

Disasters often destroy communication networks, obscure visibility, and create dangerous environments for rescuers. Traditional search missions rely heavily on manual detection and slow, unreliable information flow.

Rayvn solves this with an autonomous, self-healing rescue mesh capable of mapping survivors and hazards fully offline.

Real-World Applications

  • Disaster Response: earthquakes, floods, landslides
  • Defense & Search Ops in GPS-denied zones
  • Wildlife conservation & species monitoring
  • Industrial safety: gas leaks, heat mapping
  • Smart city underground inspection

Workflow

  • Drone Scan: Drone performs aerial survey using RGB + thermal imaging.
  • Hotspot Detection: YOLO + OpenCV detect heat signatures, motion, or human shapes.
  • Crawler Deployment: Drone deploys legged crawlers to navigate rubble and tight passages.
  • Mesh Formation: Crawlers form an autonomous LoRa mesh network.
  • Mapping & Detection: Crawlers scan areas using thermal, gas, smoke, and audio sensors.
  • Communication Link: Rescuers can initiate two-way audio communication with detected survivors via the crawler node.
  • Dashboard Sync: Data forms heatmaps and hazard clusters on the base station GUI.
  • Drone Redeploy/Exit: Depending on battery + mesh stability, drone stays, redeploys, or exits.

Core Features

  • Autonomous Pathfinding (A*)
  • Swarm coordination (drone + crawlers)
  • Thermal + camera + gas + audio sensor fusion
  • LoRa-based offline mesh network
  • AI victim detection (YOLOv8 + OpenCV)
  • Two-way audio communication (Survivors and Rescuers)
  • Live rescue dashboard with telemetry

Autonomy & Power Management

Drone Behavior States

  • Loiter Mode: Hover relay + scanning.
  • Redeploy Mode: Move to next zone.
  • Exit Mode: Return-to-base at ≤30% battery.

Crawler Battery Logic

  • >40%: exploration
  • 40–25%: relay node
  • <25%: hibernate
